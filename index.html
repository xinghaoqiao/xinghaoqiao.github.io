About me

I am an Associate Professor in the area of Innovation and Information Management at HKU Business School.
Prior to HKU, I was a tenured Associate Professor in the Department of Statistics at the London School of Economics.

I am also part of the Business Analytics Group at HKU Business School.

Education

  • MS in Statistics, University of Chicago

  • BS in Mathematics and Physics , Tsinghua University

Research interests
